EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT SUPPORT SYSTEM

This employee management support system is a software, that helps you to apply for leave, view your leave history and store your documents storage for easy retrieval. It also securely stores and manages personal and other work-related details of the employees.The system also generates leave reports. This makes it easier to store and access data when there is a need and improve employee Efficiency.
